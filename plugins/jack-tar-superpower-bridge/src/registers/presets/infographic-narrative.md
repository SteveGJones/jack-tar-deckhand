## Typography

Display: serif with restrained letter-spacing (Newsreader, Source Serif, or Crimson Pro) at 30–36 pt for titles. Body: sans-serif geometric (Inter, Source Sans) at 14–18 pt with generous line height. Straps are prose-sentence cadence, sentence-case, no terminal punctuation on slide titles. Capital letters reserved for the four cluster headings and one closing verdict line — they earn weight from scarcity.

## Layout typology

Defaults to sub-page SMARTART-FROM-LIST at the side-accent scale (~3.5 × 3.5 in, right margin alongside body prose) for evidence clusters and reasoning chains. IMAGE markers reach for the institutional schematic register — positioning maps, decision matrices, conviction-vs-drag diagrams — not photography. One BG marker permitted on the structural pivot slide; paper-grain or faint architectural margin marks at low opacity. Charts are native (CHART:slug) for every quantitative series; do not route axis-bearing subjects to IMAGE markers.

## When to reach for this register

Board reports, M&A acquisition decks, fiduciary reviews, investment-committee proposals, monetary-policy briefings, and any deck whose audience expects measured cadence + institutional gravitas. The Investigation-Verdict arc (judicial-audience pattern) is the natural narrative match. Run 6 — Velvet Ledger M&A acquisition deck — is the canonical example. When the speaker wants warm authority rather than cold corporate, this is the preset.
