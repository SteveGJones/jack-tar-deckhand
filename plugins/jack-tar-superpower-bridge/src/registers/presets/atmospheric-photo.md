# atmospheric-photo

Default strap_style: all-caps-three-beat

## Palette

| Role | Hex | Usage |
|---|---|---|
| Surface | `#0E1116` | Near-black slide background — full-bleed photographic surface |
| Structural / Primary fill | `#F5F2EC` | Bone-white for SmartArt shape fills, callout cards, dark-mode chrome |
| Text on Brand colour | `#0E1116` | Near-black text on bone shape fills |
| Body text | `#E8E4DC` | Bone-white on near-black for paragraphs |
| Accent | `#E84F2A` | Vermillion — single accent for must-earn-it-now moments only |
| Subdued slate | `#3D434B` | Cool charcoal for deferred / pruned content |
| Subtle divider | `#1E2329` | Slightly lighter than surface for low-contrast section borders |
| Marker placeholder fill | `#1A1F26` | Charcoal placeholder rectangles on the near-black surface |
| Marker placeholder border | `#5A6068` | Mid-grey dashed border on placeholder rects |

## Typography

Display: condensed grotesque (Söhne Mono, Inter Display Condensed, or Söhne Schmal) at 44–60 pt for keynote titles, ALL CAPS three-beat cadence (e.g. "BIG. BOLD. CLEAR."). Body: humanist sans (Söhne, Inter) at 16–20 pt. Title weight scales aggressively — section dividers can run 80–120 pt across a full-bleed image. Body copy is tight, photo captions are sparse.

## Layout typology

Defaults to full-bleed photographic backgrounds on title, section, and closing slides (full_bleed scale, #88). Body slides reach for sub-page IMAGE markers at the side-accent scale paired with concise prose. SMARTART-FROM-LIST is rare in this register — when used, banner scale only (~10.0 × 2.0 in, spanning content width) for linear sequences. BG markers are atmospheric textures sized to entire slide; multiple BGs are acceptable since the photographic register IS the visual language. Charts on this register are rare — when present, native CHART:slug with dark theme and single accent series.

## When to reach for this register

Conference keynotes, product launches, brand-fronted announcements, design-led decks, fundraising stories, and any setting where the visuals are themselves the message and the typography carries graphic-element weight. Audience expects sensory craft. Speaker is presenting to a room of 200+ on a wide screen. This is the antithesis of the QBR register — bring imagery, restraint, three-beat tagline cadence.
